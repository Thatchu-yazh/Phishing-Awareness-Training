PHISHING AWARENESS QUIZ

1. What is phishing?
Answer: A cyber attack used to steal sensitive information.

2. Should you click unknown email links?
Answer: No.

3. What does HTTPS indicate?
Answer: Secure website connection.

4. Is sharing OTP safe?
Answer: No.

5. Name one type of phishing attack.
Answer: Email phishing / Smishing / Vishing.
